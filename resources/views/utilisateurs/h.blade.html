Understand the Different Types of Stress

Before starting the stress test, let's explore the four main types of stress that the test will help identify.

Acute Stress

Acute stress is a short-term reaction to a specific event or situation. It usually goes away once the stressful event is over.

Chronic Stress

Chronic stress is long-term stress that lasts for a prolonged period. It can be caused by ongoing issues such as work stress, personal problems, or health concerns.

Emotional Stress

Emotional stress involves feelings of anxiety, sadness, or anger that affect your emotional well-being. It is often triggered by personal or interpersonal challenges.
